Overview

The Espresso UI framework was build to create a beautiful and professionally designed genie skin that can be applied to your business application for a rich and bold look that feels fresh and clean. Classes can be easily modified and applied to your HTML elements so that it gives you full control over the look and feel of the application. You can take the Genie skin and apply it right out of the box or full documentation has been provided to give you the ultimate control over your design.
